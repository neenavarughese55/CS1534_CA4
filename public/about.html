About Neena Varughese

Throughout my life, one of my primary hobbies has been Bharatanatyam, also known as Indian classical dance. Engaging in this art form has instilled in me the virtues of self-discipline, as I have dedicated countless hours to practise. Through perseverance, I have learned to overcome stressful situations and pay meticulous attention to the intricate hand movements that define the sophistication of each performance. Beyond dance, I find solace in listening to music, particularly pop music, to alleviate stress.

According to feedback from my peers, teachers, and self-assessment, I possess several notable strengths. Diligence is a defining trait of mine, enabling me to maintain an unwavering focus on assigned tasks. Moreover, I consistently exhibit a friendly demeanour towards those around me, fostering positive interpersonal relationships.

Looking ahead my aspirations revolve around establishing myself as a software engineer at a distinguished company. To achieve this goal, I have chosen to pursue a degree in Computing Science at the esteemed University of Aberdeen. By embarking on this educational journey, I aim to acquire a comprehensive understanding of the field and develop the necessary skills to excel in a competitive professional environment.
